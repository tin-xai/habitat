American Crow

Adult

The easiest way to separate American Crows and Fish Crows is by their calls. American Crows give a burry, full-throated “caw”, while Fish Crows are more nasal sounding, often giving short “cow” notes in pairs. American Crows are slightly larger with broader wingtips, but visual identification is very difficult.

© Henry Burton | Macaulay LibraryOregon, March 19, 2017
