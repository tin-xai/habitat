Lucy's Warbler

Adult male

Lucy's Warblers may be confused with juvenile Verdins, but the base of the bill is thinner on Lucy's than Verdin. Lucy's also have a faint eyering that juvenile Verdins lack.

© Arlene Ripley | Macaulay LibraryArizona, May 07, 2016

Female/nonbreeding male
