California Towhee

Adult

California Towhees are more rusty brown on the face rather than buffy like Canyon Towhees. California also lacks Canyon's dark central breast spot. The two species ranges have very little overlap.

Abert's Towhee

Adult

Abert's Towhees have a pale bill that contrasts with its dark face, unlike Canyon Towhee's buffy face. Abert's Towhees live mainly in brushy woodland along streams rather than desert scrub like Canyon Towhees.
