Least Tern

Breeding adult

Least Terns are smaller than Black Terns with paler back and wings. Least has a very slender yellow bill unlike Black Tern's black bill. Breeding Least Terns have a fully black cap with white forehead, whereas nonbreeding Black Tern has a small black cap surrounded by white and a dark ear spot.

Forster's Tern

Nonbreeding adult

Forster's Terns are larger and paler gray above than Black Terns, with more slender wings and a deeply forked tail. Nonbreeding Forster's has a distinct black ear patch but lacks nonbreeding Black Tern's small black cap.
