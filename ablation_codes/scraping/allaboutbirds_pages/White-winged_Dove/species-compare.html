Mourning Dove

Adult

Mourning Doves are thinner-bodied than White-winged Doves with long, tapering tails and gray-brown wings.

Eurasian Collared-Dove

Adult/immature

Eurasian Collared-Doves have a black collar across the back of the neck, lack the white wing stripe, and have a longer tail than White-winged Doves.

© Ryan Schain | Macaulay LibraryFlorida, December 12, 2015

White-tipped Dove

Adult (White-tipped)

White-tipped Doves are darker gray above than White-winged Doves and lack the bold white stripes in the wing.

© Jay McGowan | Macaulay LibraryGuárico, November 26, 2010

White-tipped Dove

Adult (White-tipped)

White-tipped Doves have small white tips to the tail, not the flashy white corners of White-winged Doves.

© Carlton Collier | Macaulay LibraryTexas, March 23, 2014

Rock Pigeon

Adult

Rock Pigeons are larger than White-winged Doves and are usually gray with dark bars in the wing, rather than brown with a white wing stripe.
