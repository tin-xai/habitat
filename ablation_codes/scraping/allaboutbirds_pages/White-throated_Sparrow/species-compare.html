Adult (Eastern)

Song Sparrows can resemble “tan-striped” White-throated Sparrows, but are much more heavily streaked below and have no yellow on the face.

White-throated Sparrow

  • Adult

    Large, plump sparrow with long tail and fairly small bill. Bold face pattern with black and white crown stripes, yellow spot between eye and bill, and neat white throat patch on gray face and breast.

    © Keenan Yakola | Macaulay LibraryMaine, May 04, 2015
  • Adult

    Large, plump sparrow with long tail and small bill. In "tan-striped" form, individuals have lower contrast head pattern with brown stripes on the head instead of white. Often found among "white-striped" individuals.

    © Ryan Schain | Macaulay LibraryMassachusetts, October 31, 2016
  • Adult

    Sings a thin whistle that sounds like Oh-sweet-canada-canada or Old-Sam-Peabody-Peabody often at the tops of trees or shrubs.

    © Benjamin Clock | Macaulay LibraryMinnesota, June 24, 2011
    Not all videos have sound
  • Immature

    Large, plump sparrow with small bill and rounded head. Immatures have brown-and-black head stripes, less yellow before the eye, and faint streaks on the breast.

    © Ryan Sanderson | Macaulay LibraryIndiana, December 09, 2019
  • Adult

    © Jonathan Irons | Macaulay LibraryMaryland, January 24, 2020
  • Adults with Dark-eyed Junco

    Readily visits feeders or pecks at fallen seeds beneath them. Eats millet and sunflower seeds.

    © Matthew D. Medler | Macaulay LibraryNew York, November 27, 2014
    Not all videos have sound
  • Adult

    Large sparrow with small grayish bill. Upperparts are reddish brown with dark brown streaks. Underparts grayish with neat white throat patch.

    © Daniel Jauvin | Macaulay LibraryQuebec, July 07, 2017
  • Adult

    White throat sometimes outline with black line. Two white wingbars on rich reddish brown wings.

    © Daniel Jauvin | Macaulay LibraryQuebec, May 13, 2017
  • Adult

    Hops along the ground, scratching at the surface to uncover insects and seeds. This video has no audio.

    © Timothy Barksdale | Macaulay LibraryOhio, May 01, 1997
    Not all videos have sound
  • Juvenile

    © Jacques Ibarzabal | Macaulay LibraryQuebec, August 11, 2018
  • Adult

    Typically found on or near the ground in forests with dense shrubby understory.

    © Anita Morales | Macaulay LibraryQuebec, July 16, 2017
  • Adult

    Often scratches at the ground with a double hop to uncover insects and seeds.

    © Chris Wood | Macaulay LibraryNew York, May 10, 2011
    Not all videos have sound

    © Michael Stubblefield | Macaulay LibraryNew Jersey, December 19, 2020
