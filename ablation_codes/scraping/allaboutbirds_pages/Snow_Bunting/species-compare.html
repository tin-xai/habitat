Lapland Longspur

Nonbreeding/immature

Nonbreeding/immature Lapland Longspurs lack the Snow Bunting's white wing patch and they have streaking on their sides that nonbreeding Snow Buntings don't have.

© Brian Sullivan | Macaulay LibraryNew Jersey, December 19, 2003

Main Species

Snow Bunting

  • Breeding male

    Chunky ground-dwelling bird with a short conical bill. Breeding males are mostly white with a black back.

    © Christoph Moning | Macaulay LibrarySvalbard, June 05, 2015
  • Breeding female

    Medium-sized full-bodied songbird with a small conical bill. Breeding females have a dark and streaky back with a dusky head and white underparts.

    © Christoph Moning | Macaulay LibrarySvalbard, June 06, 2015
  • Breeding male

    Males sing a low, husky warble on the breeding grounds.

    © Timothy Barksdale | Macaulay LibraryAlaska, June 05, 2002
    Not all videos have sound
  • Nonbreeding male

    Small-billed sparrowlike bird. Nonbreeding males are white below with rusty patches on the head, "ear," and shoulders. Its back is dark and streaky.

    © gord smith | Macaulay LibraryAlberta, January 08, 2013
  • Nonbreeding female

    Sparrowlike bird with a small conical bill. Nonbreeding females have rusty patches on the head, "ear," and chest. The back is dark with rusty streaks.

    © Caroline Lambert | Macaulay LibraryCalifornia, November 05, 2015
  • Nonbreeding female

    Forages for seeds, picking them from vegetation or from the ground.

    © Jay McGowan | Macaulay LibraryNew York, November 06, 2012
    Not all videos have sound
  • Juvenile

    Juveniles are grayish overall with darker wings and a paler belly.

    © Daniel Tinoco | Macaulay LibraryGreenland, July 21, 2015
  • Nonbreeding male

    Distinctive plumage pattern in flight. On males the outer wings are dark while the inner wings are white. The outer tail feathers also flash white in flight.

    © Max Nootbaar | Macaulay LibraryNew Jersey, December 29, 2018
  • Breeding male

    Breeds in the high arctic in rocky areas.

    © Timothy Barksdale | Macaulay LibraryNunavut, June 19, 1997
    Not all videos have sound
  • Flock

    Forms flocks with other Snow Buntings during winter where they tend to blend in with their surroundings. Forages in fields or along lake shores.

    © Sally Chisholm | Macaulay LibraryMassachusetts, January 10, 2017
  • Habitat

    Breeds in rocky areas in the tundra. Nests on the ground in rock crevices.

    © Alex Lamoreaux | Macaulay LibraryAlaska, June 22, 2017
  • Nonbreeding adult

    Tends to crouch low to the ground, blending in with the background.

    © Jay McGowan | Macaulay LibraryNew York, November 21, 2011
    Not all videos have sound
  • Flock

    © Cam Nikkel | Macaulay LibraryManitoba, January 01, 2021
