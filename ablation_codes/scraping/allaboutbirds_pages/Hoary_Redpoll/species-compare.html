Common Redpoll

Adult male

Common Redpolls are often darker overall with a larger bill than Hoary Redpolls, but there is overlap, which makes separating the paler commons from hoaries difficult. They have streaked undertail coverts and tend to have more streaking on the sides compared to Hoary Redpolls, which have unstreaked undertail coverts and lighter streaked sides.

© Ian Davies | Macaulay LibraryFinnmark, June 17, 2015

Common Redpoll

Female/immature

Common Redpolls are often darker overall with a larger bill than Hoary Redpolls, but some commons can be paler, so care should be taken with ID. They have streaked undertail coverts and more streaking on the sides compared to Hoary Redpolls, which have unstreaked undertail coverts and lightly streaked sides.

© Ryan Schain | Macaulay LibraryMassachusetts, December 23, 2012

House Finch

Adult male

House Finches are larger with a larger bill than Hoary Redpolls. Adult males have more red on the head and breast than Hoary Redpolls.

© Suzanne Labbé | Macaulay LibraryQuebec, February 21, 2017

House Finch

Female/immature

House Finches are larger with a larger bill than Hoary Redpolls. Females/immatures are darker brown and more heavily streaked than female Hoary Redpolls. They also lack the Hoary Redpoll's red crown.

© Dave Spier | Macaulay LibraryNew York, January 15, 2008

Pine Siskin

Adult (Northern)

Pine Siskins are browner overall and have a longer bill than Hoary Redpolls. Pine Siskins have yellow edging on the wings unlike juvenile Hoary Redpolls.

© Douglas Faulder | Macaulay LibraryAlberta, July 30, 2017

Hoary Redpoll

  • Adult male

    Small finch with a tiny conical bill and a notched tail. Adult males are mostly white with a red crown patch and a pink washed chest. Some individuals like this one are extremely pale with limited streaking on the sides while others are darker with more streaking.

    © Marlene Kraml | Macaulay LibraryOntario, January 28, 2021
  • Female/immature

    Small finch with a tiny yellow bill. Females/immatures are white overall with a red crown patch and lightly streaked backs and sides. Note unstreaked undertail coverts.
