American Crow

Adult

American Crows are smaller with a smaller bill than Chihuahuan Ravens. They also have a rounded or square tail, not wedge shaped like the Chihauhuan Raven.

© Henry Burton | Macaulay LibraryOregon, March 19, 2017

Common Raven

Adult

Common Ravens are larger than Chihuahuan Ravens with shorter nasal bristles and a lower voice. They also have gray bases to the body feathers not white like Chihauhaun Ravens.

© Brian Sullivan | Macaulay LibraryCalifornia, January 14, 2007
